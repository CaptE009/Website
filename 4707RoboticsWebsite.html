Team 4707 - Midway Predators

Team 4707, the Midway Predators, is a 15th generation FTC team from Waco, Texas affiliated with Midway ISD. The team was created with the purpose of inspiring and providing a place for future engineers to pursue their passions. This year, the team has 6 members, all having previous experience with robotics.

About Us

Welcome to the Midway Predators, FTC Team 4707! We are a passionate high school robotics team dedicated to innovation, teamwork, and community impact. Competing in the FIRST Tech Challenge, we tackle a new and exciting challenge each year, pushing the boundaries of engineering, programming, and strategic thinking. Our team thrives on collaboration, creativity, and a drive to succeed. Beyond the competition field, we actively engage in outreach efforts, sharing our love for STEM with our local community. From hosting workshops at libraries to mentoring middle school students, we are committed to inspiring the next generation of innovators and fostering a lifelong love of learning. We believe that robotics is more than building robots—it's about building skills, connections, and opportunities to make a difference.
